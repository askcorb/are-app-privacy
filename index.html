Privacy Policy Last Updated: October 6, 2025 Welcome to our ARE (Architect Registration Examination) study app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information. 1. Information We Collect Our app collects minimal information to provide you with study features: Study Progress: Your quiz answers, scores, and progress through exam sections are stored locally on your device only. Avatar Selection: Your chosen avatar preference is stored locally on your device. Chat Messages: When you use the "Ask CORB" AI tutor feature, your questions and conversations are sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses. These messages are not stored in our database or servers. We do NOT collect: Personal information (name, email, phone number) User accounts or login credentials Location data Analytics or usage tracking data Any information that identifies you personally 2. How We Use Your Information The limited data we handle is used solely to: Display your study progress and track which questions you've completed Remember your avatar preference Provide AI-powered tutoring responses through the CORB feature 3. Third-Party Services OpenAI API: When you use the "Ask CORB" feature, your chat messages are sent to OpenAI's servers to generate AI responses. OpenAI processes these messages according to their privacy policy. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's privacy practices at: https://openai.com/privacy We do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. 4. Data Storage and Security Local Storage: All of your study progress, quiz history, and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage methods provided by your operating system. No Cloud Storage: We do not maintain servers or cloud databases that store your personal information or study data. Chat Messages: Conversations with the AI tutor are transmitted securely to OpenAI but are not saved by us after the conversation ends. 5. Data Retention Since all data is stored locally on your device: Your data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data We cannot recover your data once the app is deleted 6. Your Privacy Rights You have complete control over your data: Access: All your data is accessible within the app Deletion: Uninstall the app to permanently delete all stored data Control: You can choose not to use the AI tutor feature if you prefer not to send messages to OpenAI 7. Children's Privacy Our app is intended for individuals preparing for professional architectural licensing and is generally used by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used our app, please contact us. 8. International Users If you use our app outside the United States, please be aware that your chat messages sent to the AI tutor feature will be transmitted to and processed by OpenAI's servers, which may be located in various countries. By using the AI tutor feature, you consent to this transfer of information. 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. 10.
